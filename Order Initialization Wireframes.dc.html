📋 Key Flows & Data Points
User Journey: 1) Upload quotation file (PDF/Excel) → 2) Enter order metadata (name, delivery date, payment status) → 3) System extracts items & checks current stock → 4) Display summary (total items, in stock, out of stock, to order)

Key Data to Show: Order name, expected delivery date, payment status (paid/unpaid), total items from quotation, items in stock count, items out of stock count, items to procure from supplier

Variant Tradeoffs:
• A (Wizard): Linear, step-by-step for less experienced users; more clicks but clear flow
• B (Compact Single): All-in-one form; faster for power users; may feel cramped
• C (Two-Column): Upload focus on left, info on right; balanced layout; requires wider screens
• D (Post-Upload Summary): Shows extraction results with metrics; good for confirmation and next actions
• E (Mobile Stack): Vertical layout; touch-friendly buttons; works on any device
