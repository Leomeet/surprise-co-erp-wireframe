Comparison

  • Option 1 (Minimal Lines): Clean & minimal. Only top/bottom separators. Feels modern. Best for quick scanning.
  • Option 2 (Zebra Stripes): Classic data table look. Alternating rows help eye tracking. Traditional but effective.
  • Option 3 (Row Spacing): Airy, modern feel. Clear separation without visual noise. Best readability. Slightly more compact than cards.
